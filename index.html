About Us

Disemart Studio is an indie art studio that transforms everyday objects into unique teardown artworks. Working with gadgets, appliances, items from daily life, and even elements inspired by nature, the studio turns familiar forms into carefully composed pieces that reveal what usually goes unseen.

Established in 2026, Disemart Studio is shaped by research, observation, and methodical breakdown. Every body of work begins with close study, understanding how something is built before deciding how it should be represented.

Traditionally, teardown is limited to technology, but Disemart Studio expands this approach further. Every object, whether produced by human hands or formed by natural processes, is made up of smaller elements, each serving a purpose beneath the exterior.

The creation process involves disassembling, cleaning, redesigning, and framing each object with precision. Through this evolving practice, Disemart Studio continues to explore new objects and ideas.

  • Conversation starters that spark curiosity
  • Learning tools that reveal inner structure and mechanics
  • Collectible pieces designed to be displayed and curated
