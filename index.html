About Us

Disemart Studio is an indie art studio that transforms everyday objects into unique teardown artworks. Working with gadgets, appliances, items from daily life, and even elements inspired by nature, the studio turns familiar forms into carefully composed pieces that reveal what usually goes unseen. Each artwork preserves the essence of its original object while presenting it as something artistic, collectible, and thought-provoking.

Established in 2026, Disemart Studio is shaped by research, observation, and methodical breakdown. Every body of work begins with close study, understanding how something is built before deciding how it should be represented. The studio values patience over speed and depth over surface, treating the creative process as an act of inquiry rather than simple visual expression.

Traditionally, teardown is limited to technology, but Disemart Studio expands this approach further. Every object, whether produced by human hands or formed by natural processes, is made up of smaller elements, each serving a purpose beneath the exterior. These structures often remain hidden and unnoticed. By carefully disassembling and revealing them, the studio brings attention to the intelligence, design, and quiet complexity behind everyday forms.

The creation process involves disassembling, cleaning, redesigning, and framing each object with precision. This transformation allows ordinary items to retain their original character while offering a fresh perspective on their construction and meaning. Through this evolving practice, Disemart Studio continues to explore new objects and ideas, bringing teardown art into homes, offices, educational spaces and curated collections.

The resulting artworks are more than decoration. They function as…

  • conversation starters that spark curiosity.
  • learning tools that reveal inner structure and mechanics.
  • collectible pieces designed to be displayed, curated, and grown into a personal series.
